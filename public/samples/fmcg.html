Cold-Pressed Variants
Started in a Pune kitchen. Stayed honest.

Zest was born in 2021 when our founder couldn't find a juice brand that didn't lie on its label. "Fresh" juices reconstituted from concentrate. "No sugar added" with fruit pulp loaded with fructose syrup. So we decided to do it differently — cold press everything in small batches, source every ingredient from named farms, and put the full ingredient list right on the label. Nothing to hide.
