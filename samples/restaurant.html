/ The Auteur

Marco Rossi

Born in Naples, Marco grew up at the elbow of his nonna — learning that Italian cooking is not a recipe, but a relationship with memory and season.

He trained formally at the Istituto Alberghiero di Napoli before earning his place under Chef Enrico Crippa at a Michelin-starred kitchen in Milan, where he spent seven formative years mastering northern technique.

In 2018, Marco arrived in Pune — drawn by its hunger for honest international cuisine. A year later, he opened Casa Bella. It was immediately full. It has been since.

He does not do tasting menus. He believes in eating well, not performing it.
